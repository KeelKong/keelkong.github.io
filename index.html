About

个人简介

I am an Associate Professor with tenure at the School of Management, University of Science and Technology of China (USTC). I received my Ph.D. in Management Science in 2020 from a Joint PhD Program between Xi'an Jiaotong University and the City University of Hong Kong. I also hold a B.A. in Business Administration from Xi'an Jiaotong University (2014).

I joined USTC as a tenure-track associate professor in 2020 and worked as a research assistant at City University of Hong Kong (2017–2020) under the supervision of Prof. Yimin Yu. I was promoted to Associate Professor with tenure in 2025.

My research interests lie in contract theory and incentive mechanism design, inventory and supply chain management, the operations–marketing interface, and behavioral operations management. My work has been published in leading journals including Operations Research, Manufacturing & Service Operations Management, Production and Operations Management, and Marketing Science.

孔祥印，中国科学技术大学管理学院副教授（固定教职）。2014年获得西安交通大学管理学学士学位，2020年获得西安交通大学与香港城市大学联合培养博士学位（管理科学方向）。

博士期间曾在香港城市大学管理科学系担任研究助理（2017–2020）。2020年加入中国科学技术大学管理学院，任特任副教授；2025年晋升为副教授（固定教职）。

主要研究方向包括：契约理论与激励机制设计、库存与供应链管理、运营与营销交叉研究以及行为运营管理。研究成果发表于 Operations Research、Manufacturing & Service Operations Management、Production and Operations Management、Marketing Science 等国际顶级期刊。
